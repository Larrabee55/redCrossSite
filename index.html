COVID-19 UPDATE: You can request a smoke alarm installation, however, we are temporarily suspending the scheduling of appointments until public health conditions improve.

Home fires claim seven lives every day but having working smoke alarms can cut the risk of death by half. The American Red Cross aims reduce home fire deaths and injuries in the United States by 25 percent over five years through the Home Fire Campaign.

At no cost we will install up to three FREE smoke alarms per home, and share valuable, lifesaving safety information. Please fill out the form below to schedule your FREE installation.

***This form can accept requests from the following states and counties: UT, NV, Uinta County WY, Lincoln County WY, Mohave County AZ.***

To learn more about the Home Fire Campaign, please visit: redcross.org/homefires
